What Makes FGS Different

Feiner Grant Strategies (FGS) assists nonprofit and for profit organizations to secure funding from government, foundations, corporations and donors. Expert grant writing and strategic coaching help clients to sustain their programs and grow their capacity so that they can serve their communities in new and better ways

FGS crafts individualized solutions for clients based on an informed understanding of their communities, constituencies, missions, programs, and the funding landscape. Building strong relationships with both clients and funders, FGS spurs organizations to set and reach high goals while helping grant makers and donors invest their funds wisely.
